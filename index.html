Overview Akhand Vasai E - Yashwant Smart City At Vasai East

At Akhand Vasai E - Yashwant Smart City, we strive to create unique lifestyle developments designed to international standards. The goal is to create a legacy by maintaining the family values for years ahead, along with focusing on a sensible life for the present. Vasai is one of the most coveted neighbourhoods in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. Yashwant Smart City has a significant location benefit due to its proximity to Vasai Road Station and Mumbai-Ahmedabad Highway. Yashwant Smart City promises to be the greatest Gateway to Mumbai for all residents.

Along with world-class modern conveniences, focusing on health-wellness through organic harvest, spending quality time with loved ones, stepping into a lush green park, trailing up a little hillock, breathing fresh air, and experiencing natural light are some actual smart living practices.
